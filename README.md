✈️ British Airways Review Analysis – Tableau Dashboard
📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing customer reviews of British Airways. The goal of this analysis is to understand passenger satisfaction across different service aspects such as cabin staff service, entertainment, food & beverages, ground service, seat comfort, and value for money.

The dashboard enables users to explore airline review data across time, countries, aircraft types, traveler types, and seat classes, helping identify patterns in customer experience.
<br> <br>
🎯 Objectives

Analyze customer feedback for British Airways services.

Identify trends in overall rating over time.

Compare ratings across countries and aircraft models.

Evaluate service components like entertainment, seat comfort, and food quality.

Enable interactive filtering for deeper insights.
<br> <br>
🛠 Tools & Technologies

Tableau – Data visualization and dashboard development

Data Analysis Techniques – Aggregation, filtering, and trend analysis

Dataset – Airline customer review dataset
<br> <br>
📌 Dashboard Features
1️⃣ Key Performance Metrics

The top section of the dashboard displays average ratings for different service categories:

Cabin Staff Service: 3.3

Entertainment: 1.4

Food & Beverages: 2.4

Ground Service: 3.0

Seat Comfort: 2.9

Value for Money: 2.8

Overall Rating: 4.2

These KPIs provide a quick overview of customer satisfaction levels.

2️⃣ Average Overall Rating by Month

A time-series line chart shows how the average rating has changed from 2016 to 2023.

Insights from this chart:

Ratings fluctuate significantly over time.

Certain months show noticeable drops in customer satisfaction.

Helps identify periods with improved or poor service performance.

3️⃣ Average Overall Rating by Country

A geographical map visualization highlights customer ratings across different countries.

Key benefits:

Identifies regions with higher or lower customer satisfaction.

Shows global distribution of airline reviews.

Useful for understanding regional customer experience.

4️⃣ Average Overall Rating by Aircraft

A horizontal bar chart compares ratings across aircraft types such as:

Airbus A350

Boeing 747

Boeing 777

Boeing 787

Airbus A320

Airbus A380

Insights:

Airbus A350 receives the highest average rating.

Some aircraft models show relatively lower customer satisfaction.

Helps understand which aircraft provide better passenger experience.
